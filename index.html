Spongebob Squarepants

Spongebob Squarepants is a yellow sponge that lives in a pineapple under the sea. He resides in the town of Bikini Bottom, working as a fry cook at the Krusty Krab.

Career

In 1999, Spongebob started his career as a fry cook at Krusty Krab. He is well known for making delicious Krabby Patties. His coworker is Squidward Tentacles and his boss is Eugene Krabs.

Personal Life

Spongebob lives alone with his pet snail Gary. He has two neighbors: his coworker, Squidward, and his best friend, Patrick Star. Spongebob is known as a local favorite, as he has many friends throughout the town. In his free time, Spongebob also dabbles in jellyfishing and karate.
